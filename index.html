About Me

My name is James Allen and I am a third-year student studying a Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) at QUT, majoring in Mechatronics, with a minor in Robotics and Aerospace. After graduating in 2016 with an OP1, I was awarded a Vice Chancellor’s Scholarship for Academic Excellence. Following this achievement I was subsequently accepted into the QUT Dean’s Scholars Program, with a current GPA of 6.813. Over the course of my degree, I have been involved in a number of exciting, challenging and rewarding projects, with these allowing me to develop and grow as an upcoming engineer. Warman Design and Build Challenge

The Warman Challenge is a yearly event run for second year Engineering students across the South East Asia Pacific region. Each year, a different challenge is presented, with teams competing for a place in the National Finals in Sydney. In 2018, my team was fortunate enough to be chosen to represent QUT at this event.
